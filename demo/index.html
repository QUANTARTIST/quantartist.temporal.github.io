GRAPPLING WITH TIME TO EXERCISE?

For Chicagoans, the answer could be that it depends on the season! We took a closer look at the trends in exercise data collected by wearable devices of individuals living in Chicago, Illinois. The below heatmap visualization represents hours of exercise per day over the course of 2016 for one of those individuals. With longer days and warmer weather in the summer, it comes as no surprise that active hours increase during the hotter months.

 
Daily Exercise Trends 2016 | Chicago, Illinois

The darker the color, the more exercise that takes place on a given day.
Each bar represents one day of 2016.
